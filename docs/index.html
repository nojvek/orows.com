ORows

ORows enables teams to curate organized knowledge-bases. It's built over the spreadsheet interface that most people are familar but goes a step further.

Most great things humanity has built is because an organized group of people worked together on a problem much larger than themselves.

You can easily define data schemas which can be nested and have relationships.

You can add rich fields such as images, files, rich text, multiselect e.t.c. You can set validations and calculated fields.

You can share only parts of data with other users, or have it view-only with comments and accept suggestions. You can even set deeply granular permissions at a row and field level. '(O_o) rows!'

If you make a mistake, or someone adds bad data, you can always audit and fix it. Version history is automatically saved.

You can create views to sort, filter, group and transform your data to share it with someone as a dashboard, report or a form.

You could even expose it as a REST API backend for your website or app. You can build a serverless website with ORows as your admin backend.

You can create management systems for projects, customers, library books, schedules, inventory, surveys e.t.c.
